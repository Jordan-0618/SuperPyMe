Establishing our Data Sources

Much of the data utilized was made available by utilizing a twitter scraping function https://github.com/igorbrigadir/twitter-advanced-search found on github. Once we recieved this information, we utilizied textBlob's machine learning sentiment analysis which has been trained on over 500,000 pieces of text.

Additionally restaurant location as well as population data was utilized to assist with identifying trends and areas that may benefit from additional restaurant locations.

As there are hundreds of restaurants and locations, we specifically looked at McDonalds, Subway, and Starbucks, as they are the three most populus chains within the US. We also kept the scope of our scraping specifically to cities within California with a population of over 75,000.
