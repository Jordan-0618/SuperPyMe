Establishing our Data Sources

Much of the data utilized was made available through the free Twitter API. Through this we were able to train a machine learning algorithm to distinguish between positive and negative tweets.

Additionally restaurant location as well as population data was utilized to assist with identifying trends and areas that may benefit from additional restaurant locations.

As there are hundreds of restaurants and locations, we pecifically looked at McDonalds, Subway, and Starbucks, as they are the three most populus chains within the US. We also kept the scope of our scraping specifically to cities within California with a population of over 75,000.
